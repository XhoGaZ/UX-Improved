Smartphone Addiction App

My task was to design a mobile app that would address smartphone use among young people. Through research, I gained insight into why young people find it such a challenge to break free from their mobile habits. I applied proven ways to deal with addiction to help users regain control over their phone usage. The design of the app aims to offer a way out of smartphone addiction through positive feedback and gamified features.

Process

This app was designed to combat the grwoing problem of uncontrollable smartphone usage. To specifically help teens who struggle with their smartphone addiction. Early on, I analyzed available options on the market and found them lacking. The main problem that I observed were that the apps felt unaspiring to use and detached from what teens are drawn to. I knew that I needed to gamify the experience and include social features that would motivate users. Features that would actually get users away from their phones and susbstitute it on healthy and rewarding activites.

Another challenge, was to get users to find the app helpful and engaging while also not promoting smarphone usage. One way I tried to combat this issue was by adding a social comepetiton section where you could compete against your friends by getting scored by what activiteis you did and how little time you spent on your smartphone.
